Working on improving radar perception as part of the University of Toronto's self-driving design team.

The Boreas Dataset is a multi-season autonomous driving dataset. I helped develop the visualization branch of the development kit and designed a serverless benchmark system for assessing model performance on the data. This work was awarded the Best Robotics Poster presentation award at Undergraduate Engineering Research Day (UnERD).

Autonomous Space Robotics Lab, UTIAS, 2021.

Open Commute is a webapp visualization of Toronto bike route density and accident frequency. Datasets from the Toronto Open Data project was collected and processed using Density Kernel Estimation and K-means clustering to identify geographic areas that pose the largest risk to Toronto cyclists.

Our sentiment analysis model won 2nd overall at Ignition Hacks, 2020 using Google's Universal Sentence Encoder to preprocess tweets.

Designed and prototyped a circuit that could simultaneously measure the input electrical power of many transducers.

Focused Ultrasound Lab, Sunnybrook Research Institute, 2019.

Worked with teams of 4 to design solutions to various challenges as part of the Praxis courses in the Engineering Science program. The Rebar Exosuit was made to help relieve musculoskeletal disorders in rebar ironworkers. The Pop-up Poncho uses the mechanics of overcurvature to create an article of rain protecting clothing with near-instant deployment. CAD design and renderings by me.
