Hi, I'm Andrew

Engineering Science Student at the University of Toronto

My goal is to design robots that will reduce the amount of dangerous and tedious tasks in our lives. To accomplish this, I hope to create methods that will enable robots to reason and act in diverse, real-world situations. My current interests are in long-horizon planning and learning representations for robotics tasks. I also don't like chores, so I'm always trying to build a robot to do them for me.
