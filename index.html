Hi, I'm Andrew

Engineering Science Student at the University of Toronto // Landscape Photographer

My goal is to design smarter autonomous robots to reduce the amount of dangerous and tedious tasks in our lives. I also just don't like chores, so I'm always trying to build a robot to do them for me. Previously, I studied digital arts and music, which I continue now by spending unhealthy amounts of time tweaking aesthetic details with headphones plugged in. Lastly, I really enjoy travelling and capturing the tranquillity of nature through my photographs.
